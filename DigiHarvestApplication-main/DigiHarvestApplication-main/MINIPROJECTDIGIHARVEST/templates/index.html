    DigiHarvest

    Farming is the practise of cultivating the soil, producing crops, and raising livestock. We take of your soil's health. We at DigiHarvest, honor the Indian Farmer's love, hard work and character. Farmers have traditionally used traditional farming techniques. These techniques were not precise, which reduced productivity and took a long time. Precise farming increases productivity by precisely determining the steps that must be performed at the appropriate time. Precision farming includes predicting weather conditions, analyzing soil, recommending crops for cultivation, and determining the amount of fertilizers and pesticides that must be used. Precise Farming collects data using advanced technologies such as IoT, Data Mining, Data Analytics, and Machine Learning.Collect data, train systems, and predict outcomes. With the assistance of technology Precise farming aids in the reduction of manual labour and the increase of productivity. Farmers have recently faced a number of challenges, including crop failure due to insufficient rainfall, soil infertility, and so on. Because of environmental changes, the proposed work assists in determining how to manage crops and harvest in an efficient manner. It directs a person's farming strategy. The goal of this work is to assist an individual in cultivating crops efficiently, resulting in high productivity at a low cost. It also aids in estimating the total cost of cultivation. This would assist an individual in pre-planning activities prior to cultivation.Hence this work focuses on the steps involved in cultivation of crop.
